Mango Avocado Salsa:

Ingredients:
2 ripe mangoes, diced
1 ripe avocado, diced
1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced
Juice of 1 lime
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
In a medium bowl, combine the diced mangoes, diced avocado, chopped red onion, minced jalapeño pepper, and chopped cilantro.
Squeeze the lime juice over the salsa and toss gently to combine.
Season the salsa with salt and pepper to taste.
Cover the salsa and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
Serve the mango avocado salsa with tortilla chips as a snack or as a topping for grilled fish or chicken. Enjoy!
